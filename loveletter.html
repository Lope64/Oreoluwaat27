My love, I love you always.

I know life has been hard, and we have both been busy grinding, but I want you to know that you are always in my heart, on my mind, and in my very soul. Words alone cannot begin to describe how much you mean to me.

Sometimes I consider myself the luckiest man on the planet. I am captivated by your love and beauty, and I cannot imagine living this life without you by my side.

While thinking of you, I came across this poem:

“All words
In the dictionaries, letters, and novels
Died.
I want to discover
A way to love you
Without words.”

Yours with all my heart – Tolulope.
